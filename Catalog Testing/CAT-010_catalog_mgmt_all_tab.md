# CAT-0010: Catalog Management – All Tab

> **Summary:** Verify that the **All** tab displays all available products regardless of category.

**Preconditions:**  
- Vendor is logged in.  
- Catalog page is accessible.  
- Products exist in multiple categories.

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Click the **All** tab. | System displays all products from every category. |
| 2 | Verify product count. | Total matches the sum of all category products. |

**Actual Result:**  
All tab displays complete product list correctly.  

**Status:**  
Pass ✅
